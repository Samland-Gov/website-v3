## Overview

The government of Samland is structured to facilitate its primary goal: experimenting with the replication of real-world governmental functions within a Minecraft environment. While currently administered by its Founder, the following conceptual structure guides its operations.

## Head of State & Administration

* **Title:** President *(Currently the Founder)*
* **Responsibilities:** Oversees all aspects of Samland's operations, sets strategic direction for experimental goals, makes key decisions regarding resource allocation, building priorities, and the interpretation of the Samlandic legislation. +
