## Overview

The government of Samland is structured to manage its operations within the Minecraft environment. The following structure guides its operations.

## Head of State & Administration

*   **Title:** President *(Currently the Founder)*
*   **Responsibilities:** Oversees all aspects of Samland's operations, sets strategic direction, makes key decisions regarding resource allocation, building priorities, and the interpretation of the Samlandic legislation.
